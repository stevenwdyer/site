Paradigm Engineering 2025

Paradigm Engineering built an autonomous go-kart to compete in the Autonomous Karting Series national grand-prix at Purdue University in June 2025.

The go-kart features a NVIDIA Jetson Orin, a Pixhawk 6C, and an ESP32-S3 microcontroller along with a suite of other devices including a GPS sensor and antenna, RC receiver, and several DC motors.

As a software developer, my contributions included:

  • Wrote ESP32-S3 firmware in C to control the drive, steering, and braking motors, as well as a watchdog for e-stop.
  • Developed tooling in Python to produce latitude and longitude .txt files from a .csv file to use as waypoints for the autonomous drive algorithm.
  • Configured Pixhawk 6C PID parameters using Mission Planner for cleaner kart control.
  • Traveled with competition team and placed second overall at the Autonomous Karting Series 2025 national grand-prix.
